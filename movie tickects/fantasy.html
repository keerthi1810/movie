Fantasy Island

The enigmatic Mr Roarke makes the secret dreams of his lucky guests come true at a luxurious but remote tropical resort, 
but when the fantasies turn into nightmares, the guests have to solve the island's mystery in order to escape with their lives.
Initial release: 6 February 2020 (Nigeria)

 Director: Jeff Wadlow
 Box office: $47.1 million
 Budget: 70 lakhs USD
 Producers: Jeff Wadlow, Jason Blum, Marc Toberoff
