Lootcase

Nandan Kumar comes across a suitcase and discovers it is full of cash. He knows nothing about who it belongs to, 
where it's from, or how it got there. Nandan has to decide whether or not to take it or leave it.

 Director: Rajesh Krishnan
 Production company: Fox Star Studios
 Cinematography: Sanu Varghese
 Music director: Rohan-Vinayak, Sameer Uddin, Amar Mangrulkar
