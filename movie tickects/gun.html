Guns of Banaras

Guns of Banaras is an Indian Hindi language action film directed by Sekhar Suri and starring Karan Nath 
and Nathalia Kaur in leading roles and Zarina Wahab, Vinod Khanna, Shilpa Shirodkar, Abhimanyu Singh and 
Tej Sapru in supporting roles. Karan Nath makes a comeback with this film after 11 years. 

 Initial release: 28 February 2020
